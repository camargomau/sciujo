_**(definition)**_

An _asymmetric digraph_ is a digraph that:

- Has more than one pair of [[Parallel Arcs|parallel arcs]].
- Has at least one arc that is non-[[Recurring Arcs|recurring]].
- Has no [[Loop|loops]].
