_**(definition)**_

Given two [[Vector|vectors]] $u$ and $v$, the [[Angle|angle]] between them is given by:

$$
\cos \theta = \frac{u \cdot v}{\|u\| \|v\|}
$$

_**(theorem)**_

Notice that $u$ and $v$ are [[Orthogonal|orthogonal]] when $v \cdot w = 0$.
