_**(definition)**_

Given $X$ a [[Variable (Statistics)|variable]] and $k(X)$ its [[Kurtosis|kurtosis]], its _excess kurtosis_ (also known as _Fisher's coefficient_) is defined as:

$$
k_{3}(X) = k(X) - 3
$$
