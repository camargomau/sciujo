_**(definition)**_

Given $r(t)$ a [[Vector-Valued Function|vector-valued function]], we define the _tangent unit vector_ $T(t)$ as:

$$
T(t) := \frac{r'(t)}{\|r'(t)\|}
$$

The tangent unit vector helps us define the [[Normal Unit Vector|normal unit vector]].
