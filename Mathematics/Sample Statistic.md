_**(definition)**_

A _sample statistic_ or _statistic_ (_estadÃ­stico_ in [[Spanish Language|Spanish]]) is a [[Random Variable|random variable]] of the form $g(X_{1}, \dots, X_{n})$ where $X_{1}, \dots, X_{n}$ is a [[Random Sample|random sample]] and $g : \mathbb{R}^{n} \to \mathbb{R}$ a [[Function|function]].

For instance, in the context of a sample, [[Mean|mean]] and [[Variance|variance]] are sample statistics.
