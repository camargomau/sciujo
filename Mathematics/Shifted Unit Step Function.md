_**(definition)**_

The **shifted [[Unit Step Function|unit step function]]** is defined as:

$$
U(t-a) =
\begin{cases}
0 & \text{if } t \leq a \\
1 & \text{if } t > a
\end{cases}
$$

As the name suggests, it's just a unit step function that has been shifted from $0$ to $a$.
