Let $l_1 = \{ p + tu : t \in \mathbb{R} \}$ and $l_2 = \{ q + sv : v \in \mathbb{R} \}$ be [[Line|lines]] in $\mathbb{R}^3$.

The distance between $l_1$ and $l_2$ is given by:

$$
\text{d}(l_1, l_2) = \frac{|\ (p-q) \cdot (u \times v)\ |}{\| u \times v \|}
$$
