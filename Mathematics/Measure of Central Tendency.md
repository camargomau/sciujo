_**(definition)**_

A _measure of central tendency_ (or simply _central tendency_) is a value in a [[Set|set]] of data that, when sorted, is found in the centre of it.

There are three main types of measures of central tendency:

- Mean
	- [[Arithmetic Mean]]
	- [[Weighted Mean]]
- [[Median]]
- [[Mode]]
