_**(definition)**_

Two [[Arc|arcs]] $v_1$ and $v_2$ are _recurring_ if the initial [[Vertex|vertex]] of $v_1$ is the final vertex of $v_2$, and the final vertex of $v_1$ is the initial vertex of $v_2$.

Compare [[Parallel Arcs|parallel arcs]].
