_**(definition)**_

In [[Graph|graph]] $G$, the _closed neighbourhood_ of a vertex $v$, denoted $N[v]$, is given by:

$$
N(v) \cup \{v\}
$$

In other words, it's the union of $v$'s [[Open Neighbourhood|open neighbourhood]] and $v$ itself.
