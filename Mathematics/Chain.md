_**(definition)**_

A **chain** is a [[Stochastic Process|stochastic process]] with both a discrete [[State Set|state set]] and a [[Parameter Set|parameter set]]

Sequences of random variables are also sometimes simply called **stochastic series with a discrete state set**.
