_**(definition)**_

Let $x_{1}, \dots, x_{n}$ be observations of a [[Variable (Statistics)|variable]] $X$.

We defined the _weighted mean_ as:

$$
\overline{X} = \frac{\sum_{i=1}^n w_{i} x_{i} }{ \sum_{i=1}^n w_{i} }
$$
