_**(lemma)**_

If a [[Graph|graph]] with no loops has $e$ [[Edge|edges]] and $n$ [[Vertex|vertices]], then the sum of its [[Vertex Degree|vertices' degrees]] is twice the number of edges.

That is:

$$
\sum_{i=1}^n d(v_i) = 2e
$$
