_**(definition)**_

Let $r(t) : \mathbb{R} \to \mathbb{R}^n$ be a [[Vector-Valued Function|vector-valued function]].

We say that $r(t)$ is _continuous_ at $a$ when, given its [[Limit of a Vector-Valued Function|limit]] as $t \to a$:

$$
\lim_{t \to a} r(t) = r(a)
$$
