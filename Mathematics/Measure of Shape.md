A _measure of shape_ is a value that helps us describe the "shape" of a [[variable]]'s mass.

The most common measures of shape are:

- [[Moment#As a Measure of Shape|Moments]]
- [[Skewness]]
- [[Kurtosis]]
- [[Excess Kurtosis|Fisher's coefficient]]
