_**(definition)**_

An [[Estimator|estimator]] $\hat{\theta}$ of parameter $\theta$ is said to be _unbiased_ if:

$$
\mathbb{E}[ \hat{\theta} ] = \theta
$$

(see [[Expected Value|expected value]])
