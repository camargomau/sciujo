_**(definition)**_

Let $F: A \to B$ be a [[Function|function]] and let $C \subseteq A$. The _image_ of $C$ is defined as:

$$
\text{im}(C) = \{ F(a) \in B \mid a \in C \}
$$

Now, let $E \subseteq B$. The preimage of $E$ is:

$$
F^{-1}(E) = \{a \in A \mid F(a) \in E \}
$$
